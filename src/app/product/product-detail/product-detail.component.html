Steamer (Couscoussier)

Reference : 215546

Area of Use Our Hand-hammered Copper Couscoussier consists of two pots; a perforated top pot to steam couscous, rice or vegetable, while cooking meat or even boil pasta in the bottom pot. Materials: The Copper main core provides an excellent heat conduction and distribution around the entire surface of the cookware, while the tin hand-coated interior allows healthy cooking and non reactivity with acidic foods. Aesthetic: Its hand-hammered finish and shine-polished Brass casted Handle make it strikingly beautiful to serve in or to decorate the kitchen.
